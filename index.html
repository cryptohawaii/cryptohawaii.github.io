What are Crypto Currencies?

Any form of currency that only exists digitally (e.g. Bitcoin, Ethereum, EOS, etc), that usually has no central issuing or regulating authority but instead uses a decentralized system to record transactions and manage the issuance of new units, and that relies on cryptography to prevent counterfeiting and fraudulent transactions

Who Can Buy?

A pilot program will allow virtual asset service providers to do business in the state of Hawaii without obtaining a money transmitter license for a two-year period. This 2 year period started in the first half of 2020.

Improving Hawaii's Future

Cryptocurrency and digital currency opens many doors for financial technology or fintech in Hawaii. Fintech has huge potential and the ability to improve and stabilize Hawaii's economy especially after the hardships brought about by the corona virus or COVID19
