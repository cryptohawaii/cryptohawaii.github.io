Reputable Cryptocurrency Exchanges

  1. Coinbase

    Coinbase is a leading U.S.-based cryptocurrency exchange with over 108 million users as of 2025. It offers a user-friendly platform for buying, selling, and storing various cryptocurrencies. Coinbase supports a wide range of digital assets and provides educational resources for beginners. Learn more.

  2. Robinhood Crypto

    Robinhood Markets provides commission-free trading of cryptocurrencies alongside traditional financial instruments. With 24.3 million funded accounts and $152 billion in assets under custody, Robinhood offers a seamless experience for users looking to diversify their portfolios. Learn more.

  3. Crypto.com

    Crypto.com is a global platform offering a comprehensive suite of cryptocurrency services, including trading, staking, and a crypto wallet. As of June 2023, it boasts over 80 million users worldwide. The platform also offers a Visa card that allows users to spend their cryptocurrencies seamlessly. Learn more.

  4. BitMart

    BitMart is a global digital asset trading platform supporting over 1,400 cryptocurrencies and tokens. Registered in the Republic of Seychelles, it operates in 180 countries and offers services such as spot trading, futures trading, and staking. Learn more.

  5. Bitget

    Bitget has experienced significant growth, expanding its user base from 20 million in January 2024 to 100 million by December 2024. The platform offers various trading options, including spot and futures trading, and has received regulatory approvals in multiple jurisdictions. Learn more.

Regulatory Landscape in Hawaii

Hawaii has made significant progress in cryptocurrency regulation. The state has implemented a digital currency sandbox, allowing companies to operate without a money transmitter license, fostering innovation while ensuring consumer protection. Residents can now access and use major cryptocurrency platforms that comply with state regulations.
